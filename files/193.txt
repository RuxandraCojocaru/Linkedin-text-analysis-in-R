To support our extraordinary teams who build great products and contribute to our growth we’re looking to add a Compensation & Benefits Analyst located in Flex Timisoara.

Reporting to the Total Reward Support Team Leader the Compensation & Benefits Analyst role involves data processing data verification and support for the EMEA Flex colleagues. In this role you will collaborate with both colleagues and third parties and perform your work in a dynamic people oriented team.

What a typical day looks like:

You will draw up reports and analysis related to Compensations & Benefits activity;
You will work together with the local regional and global team in salary increase and bonus processes;
Drawing up periodical reports consolidate analyses and provide needed information for specific market studies at corporate EMEA and HR local department levels;
You will design new reports and update the existing ones in order to fulfill the requirements of the end users;
Inputing data into global and local systems;
You will work together with regional and global HR Compensations & Benefits department;
Solving the requests of the internal customers according to agreed procedures and Service Level Agreements (SLAs);
Taking part in different corporate projects of organizational development acting for the rolling out of projects reports data consolidations analysis;
Continuously work to the improvement of the KPIs and processes;

The experience we’re looking to add to our team:

Minimum experience in Human Resources / Compensations & Benefits;
Working experience in HR Shared Services or customer service is an advantage;
Proficient English skills – as a mandatory prerequisite;
Advanced MS Office skills: Excel Word or PowerPoint;
Analytical spirit well organized personality;
Fast learning ability and adaptability;
Excellent verbal and written communication skills;
Ability to prioritize and multitask;
Any other foreign language is a plus (Italian German Hungarian etc.);
